Achillea 'Coronation Gold'

Common name: sort i röllikesläktet, yarrow 'Coronation Gold'
Family: Asteraceae subfam. Asteroideae (aster family)
Hardiness: A* - Tough & tolerates Swedish winters well in a well-drained location.
Life form: Herbaceous perennial
Usage: Perennials
Diseases: May suffer from powdery mildews.
Pests: Can get aphids.
Pruning: Cut back untidy foliage in spring.
Horticultural merit: RHS Award of Garden Merit
