Diervilla lonicera

Common name: getris, bush honeysuckle
Family: Caprifoliaceae
Distribution: Exposed rocky sites and forests
Habitat: North America
Hardiness: Zone 4, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous shrub/sub-shrub
Usage: Shrubs; Groundcovering/low-growing shrubs
Size: H: 80-100 cm; W: 80-100 cm
Design: Solitaire or in a group. Urban and school environments, ground cover and underplanting in parks. Beautiful autumn colors.
Cultivation: Sun-partial shade in well-drained soil. Withstands salt, exhaust fumes, wind and drought well.
Description: Low shrub with beautiful leaf colours that cope well with urban environments.
