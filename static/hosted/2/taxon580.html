Prunus pumila var. depressa E

Common name: sandkörsbär, prostrate sand cherry, Flat sand cherry
Family: Rosaceae (Rose family)
Distribution: C & E North America
Habitat: Rocky and sandy lake shores
Hardiness: Zone 5, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous shrub/sub-shrub
Usage: Groundcovering/low-growing shrubs
Design: Ground cover for sunny, lean and well-drained surfaces that provide both spring bloom and autumn color.
Size: H: 0,25 m
Cultivation: Sunny places. Prefers well draining, less fertile soils
Horticultural merit: E-planta
Description: Low, creeping shrub for dry lean locations. White flowers and dark green leaves that take on a reddish autumn color.
