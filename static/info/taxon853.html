Viburnum farreri

Common name: kejsarolvon, Farrer viburnum
Family: Adoxaceae (moschatel family)
Distribution: Asia; NC China; Qinghai, Xinjiang
Hardiness: Zone 3, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous shrub/sub-shrub
Usage: Shrubs
Description: V. farreri is a large, upright deciduous shrub becoming more rounded with age, with prominently veined oval leaves, coppery when young. Clusters of fragrant white flowers, 5cm in width, open from pink buds from late autumn
