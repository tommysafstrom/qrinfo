Cotoneaster dammeri 'Major'

Common name: krypoxbär 'Major', bearberry cotoneaster 'Major'
Family: Rosaceae (Rose family)
Distribution: East Asia: China
Habitat: Mountainous regions on cliffs and in open mixed forests
Hardiness: Zone 4, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Evergreen shrub/sub-shrub
Usage: Groundcovering/low-growing shrubs
Size: H: 5-15 cm; W: 2 m.
Design: Tough ground cover plant suitable for slopes, among other things.
Leaf: Round, shiny, evergreen.
Fruit: Red berries.
Cultivation: Sun-partial shade in well-drained moisture-retaining soil. Can handle many different types of soil.
Description: Low evergreen ground cover for tough locations.
