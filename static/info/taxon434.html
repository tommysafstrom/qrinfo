Magnolia stellata 'Royal Star'

Common name: stjärnmagnolia 'Royal Star', star magnolia 'Royal Star'
Family: Magnoliaceae (magnoliaväxter)
Distribution: Eastern Asia: Japan
Habitat: Streambanks in moist soils
Hardiness: Zone 3, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous shrub-tree
Usage: Large Shrubs/Tree-like shrubs
Size: H: 0,7-1,5 m; B: 1-2 m
Design: Passar i trädgårds- och parkmiljö.
Cultivation: Sun to partial shade, in moisture-retaining, well-drained humus-rich, slightly acidic soil. Can handle light alkaline soils if the water holding capacity is good.
Horticultural merit: RHS Award of Garden Merit (AGM)
Description: Large deciduous shrub with widespread rounded growth and with pink flower buds and large white fragrant flowers in spring.
