Taxon

Fagus sylvatica Purpurbladiga Group

Common name: blodbok, copper beech
Family: Fagaceae (Beech family)
Distribution: Europe: Sweden to Italy, Spain to Turkey
Habitat: Woodlands with well drained soils
Hardiness: Zone 4, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous tree
Usage: Large trees
Size: H: 20-25 (-30)m; W: 15-20 m.
Cultivation: Sunny location on well-drained moisture-retaining soil.
Design: Large tree for parks.
Hedge: Commonly used as hedge
Description: Large trees that are most beautiful in sunny locations. Usually seed propagated.
