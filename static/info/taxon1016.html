Laburnum × watereri 'Vossii'

Common name: hybridgullregn 'Vossii', Voss's laburnum
Family: Fabaceae subfam. Faboideae (Pea family)
Hardiness: Zone 3, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous tree
Usage: Large Shrubs/Tree-like shrubs
Size: H: 4-8 m; W: 4-8 m.
Design: Solitaire tree or to be used against walls or trained for pergolas/espaliers.
Cultivation: Sun-partial shade. Can handle many different soils as long as they are well drained. The whole plant is poisonous.
Horticultural merit: RHS Award of Garden Merit (AGM)
Description: Litet lövfällande träd eller stor buske med bred krona och klargula blomklasar. Hela växten giftig.
