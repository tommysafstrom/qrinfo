Prunus 'Accolade'

Common name: prydnadskörbär 'Accolade', cherry 'Accolade'
Family: Rosaceae (Rose family)
Hardiness: Zone 3, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous tree
Usage: Trees
Size: H: 5-8 m; W: 5-8 m
Design: Ornamental tree, used in Kungsträdgården in Stockholm, among other places.
Cultivation: Sunny location. Does not tolerate compacted soil but otherwise quite undemanding. Withstands wind well.
Horticultural merit: RHS Award of Garden Merit (AGM)
Description: A small tree, 3-5 m tall, vase-shaped when young, becoming broader with more horizontal branches over time. Grown for its rich flowering on bare twigs with double light pink flowers in clusters during April-May. Yellow-orange-red colors in fall.
