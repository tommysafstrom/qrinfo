Symphoricarpos × chenaultii 'Hancock'

Common name: hybridsnöbär 'Hancock', snowberry 'Hancock'
Family: Caprifoliaceae
Hardiness: Zone 3, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous shrub/sub-shrub
Usage: Shrubs
Size: H: 0.5-1 m B: 2 m
Design: Backbone plant, free-growing. Plant under trees that provide light shade
Flower: Små klocklika vitrosa blommor i juni-juli
Cultivation: Sun to shade
Description: Shiny foliage, pink-colored shoots, small bell-like white-pink flowers in June-July, small white fruits that turn pink, yellow autumn color.
