Taxon

Cotinus coggygria (Rubrifolius Group) 'Royal Purple'

Common name: rödbladig perukbuske 'Royal Purple', smoke tree 'Royal Purple'
Family: Anacardiaceae (cashew family)
Distribution: Southern Europe to East Asia
Habitat: Dry hillsides, rocky places and open woods
Hardiness: Zone 2, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Deciduous shrub/sub-shrub
Usage: Large Shrubs/Tree-like shrubs
Design: As a solitaire in a sunny warm location.
Size: H: 2-3 (- 8) m; W: 1-2 (-8) m
Cultivation: Grow in sun or partial shade in a well-drained soil
Horticultural merit: RHS Award of Garden Merit (AGM)
Description: Large bushy eye-catching shrub for sheltered locations.
