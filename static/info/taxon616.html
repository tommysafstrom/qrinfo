Rhododendron (Caucasicum Group) 'Cunningham's White'

Common name: caucasium-rododendron, rhododendron 'Cunningham's White'
Family: Ericaceae
Hardiness: Zone 4, Swedish hardiness zone map for woody plants
Life form: Evergreen shrub/sub-shrub
Usage: Shrubs
Size: Höjd: 2,4-4 m (1-5-2 m i Sv.). Bredd: 2-4 m.
Growth habit - form: Robust
Design: Park- och trädgårdsmiljöer.
Flower: White with a yellow centre. May-June. Buds pink.
Cultivation: Halvskugga i jämnt fuktig sur jord.
Description: A robust evergreen shrub with large clusters of white flowers.
